    Synopsis

    The story of our game tells about a couple in New Hampshire who, after a long day of walking, decide to spend the night in the only hotel in the small town of Whispy Woods. After collecting their key from reception, they go to their room on the third floor to rest, room 364. From then on, strange things start to happen. A thud knocks on their door. Opening the door, the protagonists realize that no one is there. Suddenly, the upstairs lights start to crackle and then go out. Only one small light remains on in room 364. Determined to report the problem to reception, they set out to find a flashlight in their belongings. As they step out into the deserted corridor on the third floor, they notice that the elevator, the only way down to reception, has broken down due to the power failure. Stranded, they decide to find a way of restoring it themselves by exploring the floor...
